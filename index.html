History

Chinese cuisine all started with a 4 thousand year old archaelogical find of the oldest noodle found in the upper parts of the Chinese Yellow River. The development of grain during the Han dynasty allowed for cooking to develop too. Specialization of Chinese cuisine then developed during the Tang Dynasty, when tea also rose in popularity as if signified wealth and balance. It was during the Song Dynasty when trade flourished, making cooking more accessible to people, allow Chinese cuisine to blossom.
